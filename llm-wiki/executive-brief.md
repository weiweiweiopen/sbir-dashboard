# Executive Brief

## Current best strategic frame

The project should be framed as a **patterned DSSC material-identity system**, not as a generic PUF, blockchain, solar-cell, or light-field project.

The strongest integrated thesis is:

> A patterned dye-sensitized solar cell (DSSC) can act as a material entropy source. Under controlled illumination, its I-V curve and derived features can reconstruct a device identity. Registered identities can support verification and event logging, with optional external hash anchoring.

## What to make visible

- patterned DSSC fabrication variance;
- I-V curve measurement protocol;
- repeatability across illumination conditions;
- separability between cells;
- panel ID registration and verification;
- material event signature log.

## What to keep secondary

- light-field / lensless imaging;
- micro-lens efficiency enhancement;
- blockchain / ledger anchoring;
- general IoT trust architecture;
- generic optical PUF language.

## Highest-value next work

1. Build a repeatability / separability dataset.
2. Define feature vectors and thresholds.
3. Produce a claim chart against the known patent clusters.
4. Draft a provisional claim tree around the DSSC + I-V + event-signature workflow.
5. Keep public repo data project-level and privacy-clean.
